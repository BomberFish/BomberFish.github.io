Data we collect

The amount of data the app sends can be directly changed from its settings. There are three options to choose from: Full, Limited, and None (also known as Disabled).

ㅤ

Full

  • Device Model
  • Device iOS Version
  • Whether it is your first launch or not
  • Language Region
  • Anonymized crash logs, ONLY if you click "report to developer".
  • App version (for updates)

Limited
  • App version (for updates)
  • Whether it is your first launch or not

None/Disabled

  • No data is collected. The app will only contact the server to check for updates if the toggle is enabled in its settings.



How your data is used

Data collected is only used for user statistics and error reporting.
